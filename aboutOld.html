Who am I ?

Most people call me Percy, I get called Percival when I'm in trouble.
Im a 49 year old Front End Web Developer and UI designer based in the Vancouver BC area. I'm a father of two beautiful children. Brooklyn is 7 years old and is loving second grade. Our newest edition to the family is Zachary. He's 6 months young and is a very happy little boy who loves his mushy peas.
I'm a veteran of 27yrs from a place called COSTCO. You may have heard of it.
I had the opportunity to make the leap into the tech field and I decided to study Web Development.

Im a recent graduate of Red Academy. I studied the Web Developer professional as well as the UI communication programs there.

As a UI designer, I am competent with color theory, composition/space, design thinking/research, branding systems, logo design, client relations and public speaking to name a few.
I made a choice to learn UI design and sought to become a well rounded technologist. Armed with coding skills, I feel I'm a valuable asset to any organization as I can not only design web/app elements, but I can also code them and bring them to life.
While working in either roll I bridge the communication/relationship gap between the teams.

My passion lies as a coder. I love to write code that has awesome functionality and to make apps that enrich peoples lives. I love to design, but I think coding gives me the greatest satisfaction. Currently I'm studying React js. I'm finding that its a smooth transition, as the react library and syntax are familiar to what I currently know. Also, working in teams using the Agile software development process was eye opening to me. It has really opened my eyes into a whole new world of creating new projects. Im currently looking for employment, but I'm excited about growing with a company that's going to nurture my thirst for knowledge and help me become the best developer that I can be.

Skies the limit!

Wish me luck on a new adventure !

Percy.
