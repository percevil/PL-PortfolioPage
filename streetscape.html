StreetScape

A free form website based on a car enthusiast festival

  • see a demo

StreetScape was my final project in the UI program at Red. It is a web site design project based on a
festival or concert event idea. I chose to do a car enthusiast event that showcased appearances by car
scene celebrities, food, concerts and other entertaining experiences. The design is still some what rough
as my wife was in the middle of labour and I had to complete the final iteration in just 9 days from the ground up.
