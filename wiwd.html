My name is Percival, Percy for short.
Im a 49 year old web developer and UI designer based in the Vancouver BC area. I'm a father of two kids. Brooklyn is 7 years old and is loving second grade. Our newest editon to the family id Zachary. He is 6 months young and is a very happy little boy who loves to eat his vegetables in mushy form.

Im a recent graduate of the Red Academy. I studied the Web Developer as well as the UX/UI programs there.

As a UI designer, I am competent with color theory, composition/space, design thinking/research, branding systems, logo design, client relations and public speaking to name a few.
I made a choice to learn UI design and sought to become a well rounded technologist. Armed with coding skills, I feel I'm a valuable asset to any organization as I can not only design web/app elements, but I can also code them and bring them to life.
While working in either roll I bridge the communication/relationship gap within the teams.

My passion lies as a coder. I love to write code that has awesome functionality and to make apps that enrich peoples lives. I love to design, but I think coding gives me the greatest satisfaction.
